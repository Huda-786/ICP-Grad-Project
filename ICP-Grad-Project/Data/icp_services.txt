ICP SERVICES KNOWLEDGE BASE
=============================

SERVICE 1: ISSUANCE OF A NEW ID CARD
--------------------------------------
Description:
A service whereby an ID card is issued for the first time to an individual who has not previously registered or been issued an ID number.

Conditions:
- Service is mandatory for UAE Nationals, GCC Nationals, and UAE Residents
- Residents must have a valid residence permit
- Passport must be valid for more than 6 months
- Biometric requirements such as fingerprint and signature must be completed

Required Documents:
- Personal photo (4.5 x 3.5 cm, white background)
- Passport copy (for residents and GCC nationals)
- Original agency certificate if someone applies on behalf of the applicant
- Birth certificate and the passport of one of the parents or 
the Emirates ID card for children under 15
- Valid residence permit for residents

Additional Documents based on the category:
CATEGORY 1: LEARNERS
----------------------
Required Document:
A certificate stating the continuation of study or admission to one of the universities, colleges, institutes or state public or private schools issued by the Ministry of Education or a local educational institution.
OR
A certificate stating the continuation of study or admission to one of the governmental or private centers for people with special needs issued by the Ministry of Community Development or the competent local authority.


CATEGORY 2: EMPLOYEES
-----------------------
Required Documents (one of the following):
1. Original or a copy of the approved electronic extract or a copy of the employment contract issued by the Ministry of Human Resources and Emiratisation, the governmental or semi-governmental institution, or the approved electronic document issued by the free zones in the State.
2. Original of a work certificate from the governmental or semi-governmental institutions.
3. Original of the job offer issued by the governmental or semi-governmental entity.

Special Cases:
- Workers in offices of sheikhs: Replace requirement 1 with an original letter of continuity of work or acceptance of work from the office of the sheikh.
- Gulf customer or legal representative of institutions/companies: Replace requirement 1 with the original power of attorney (duly certified if issued from outside the country).
- Employees and players in sports clubs affiliated to the Sports Council of the Emirate: Replace requirement 1 with an original business continuity letter issued by the Executive Director of the Sports Club.
- Retired Gulf customer or deceased Gulf retiree receiving pension: A letter from the authority concerned with disbursing the pension is required.


CATEGORY 3: INVESTORS
----------------------
Required Document (one of the following):
- An original valid commercial, industrial or agricultural license.
- Electronic document issued and approved by the economic development departments or free zones in the State.
- Original contract for the purchase of real estate from developers from the public or private sector.


CATEGORY 4: PROPERTY OWNERS
-----------------------------
Required Document (one of the following):
- Original ownership of the property (land or building).
- Original certificate of granting housing / usufruct of housing / allocation of government housing (permanent or temporary) issued by the competent government authority in the Emirate.
- Original of the final ruling to the determination of heirs (for cases of registration of real estate heirs).


CATEGORY 5: SCHOLARS / DELEGATES
----------------------------------
Required Document:
A certificate issued by the government or semi-government authority in the country (local/Gulf/international) or banks operating in the country organizing or hosting the official mission, training course or hosting the delegated Gulf national.


CATEGORY 6: INMATES IN CORRECTIONAL AND PENAL FACILITIES
----------------------------------------------------------
Required Document:
A certificate from courts or the correctional and penal institutions in the State stating that the customer is serving a sentence in accordance with a judicial ruling issued against him/her.


CATEGORY 7: KINSHIP WITH A GULF CITIZEN STUDYING IN THE COUNTRY
-----------------------------------------------------------------
Eligible relatives: First-degree (father, mother, husband/wife, children), brother or sister.

Required Documents:
1. Original valid Emirates ID for the Gulf Citizen who studies in the country.
2. Certificate stating the continuation of study or admission to a university, college, institute, public or private school issued by the Ministry of Education or local educational institution. OR certificate from a center for people with special needs issued by the Ministry of Community Development.
3. Proof of kinship: original birth certificate, original Gulf state document equivalent to a family book, original marriage contract, or certificate of proof of lineage or dependency (documents issued outside the country must be duly certified).


CATEGORY 8: KINSHIP WITH A GULF CITIZEN WORKING IN THE COUNTRY
----------------------------------------------------------------
Eligible relatives: Second-degree (first degree: father, mother, husband/wife, children / second degree: grandfather, grandmother, brother, sister, grandchild, granddaughter).

Required Documents:
1. Original valid Emirates ID for the Gulf citizen who works in the country.
2. Original or approved electronic copy of employment contract, work certificate, or job offer issued by the Ministry of Human Resources and Emiratisation or governmental/semi-governmental institution or free zone.

Special Cases:
- Workers in offices of sheikhs: Replace requirement 2 with original letter of continuity or acceptance of work from the sheikh's office.
- Legal representative of institutions/companies: Replace requirement 2 with original power of attorney (duly certified if issued from outside the country).
- Sports club employees/players: Replace requirement 2 with original business continuity letter from the Executive Director of the Sports Club.
- Retired Gulf customer or deceased Gulf retiree receiving pension: A letter from the pension disbursing authority is required.

3. Proof of kinship: original birth certificate, original Gulf state document equivalent to a family book, original marriage contract, or certificate of proof of lineage or dependency (documents issued outside the country must be duly certified).


CATEGORY 9: KINSHIP WITH AN INVESTOR GULF CITIZEN (LICENSE OR PROPERTY)
-------------------------------------------------------------------------
Eligible relatives: Second-degree (first degree: father, mother, husband/wife, children / second degree: grandfather, grandmother, brother, sister, grandchild, granddaughter).

Required Documents:
1. Original valid Emirates ID for the Gulf national investor.
2. Original valid commercial, industrial or agricultural license OR electronic document from economic development departments or free zones OR original real estate purchase contract.
3. Proof of kinship: original birth certificate, original Gulf state document equivalent to a family book, original marriage contract, or certificate of proof of lineage or dependency (documents issued outside the country must be duly certified).


CATEGORY 10: KINSHIP WITH A GULF CITIZEN WHO IS A PROPERTY OWNER
------------------------------------------------------------------
Eligible relatives: Second-degree (first degree: father, mother, husband/wife, children / second degree: grandfather, grandmother, brother, sister, grandchild, granddaughter).

Required Documents:
1. Original valid Emirates ID for the Gulf national property owner.
2. Original property ownership document (land or building) OR certificate of granting housing/usufruct/government housing allocation OR final ruling for determination of heirs.
3. Proof of kinship: original birth certificate, original Gulf state document equivalent to a family book, original marriage contract, or certificate of proof of lineage or dependency (documents issued outside the country must be duly certified).


CATEGORY 11: KINSHIP WITH A DECEASED UAE CITIZEN
--------------------------------------------------
Eligible relatives: First-degree only (father, mother, husband/wife, children).

Required Documents:
1. A copy or true copy of the Emirates ID card or passport of the deceased UAE national OR the death certificate of the deceased UAE national.
2. Proof of kinship: original birth certificate, original marriage contract, or certificate of proof of lineage or dependency (documents issued outside the country must be duly certified).


CATEGORY 12: KINSHIP WITH A UAE CITIZEN
-----------------------------------------
Eligible relatives: Third-degree (first degree: father, mother, husband/wife, children / second degree: grandfather, grandmother, brother, sister, grandchild, granddaughter / third degree: uncle and aunt).

Required Documents:
1. Original valid Emirates ID for the UAE citizen.
2. For third-degree kinship: a certificate of support issued by the court is required.
3. Proof of kinship: original birth certificate, original marriage contract, or certificate of proof of lineage or dependency (documents issued outside the country must be duly certified).


CATEGORY 13: KINSHIP WITH A RESIDENT FOREIGNER
------------------------------------------------
Eligible relatives: First-degree (father, mother, husband/wife, children).

Required Documents:
1. Original valid passport and valid Emirates ID for the resident who wishes to register the relative.
2. Proof of kinship: original birth certificate, original marriage contract, or certificate of proof of lineage or dependency (documents issued outside the country must be duly certified).


CATEGORY 14: GULF GUARDIAN, RESIDENT, CUSTODIAN OR CHILD IN CUSTODY
---------------------------------------------------------------------
Required Documents:
1. Valid original passport and valid Emirates ID for the Gulf Guardian, Resident, Custodian or child in custody.
2. Original proof issued by the court stating that the Gulf customer is the agent, resident or guardian.
3. For the child in custody category: a document from the court stating the child is under the custody of a Gulf national or a foreigner residing in the country (court document must be duly certified if issued from outside the country).

Fees:
- UAE Citizens (5 years): AED 100
- UAE Citizens (10 years): AED 200
- GCC Nationals: AED 100
- Residents: AED 100 per year
- E-Services fee: AED 100
- Urgent service fee: AED 150
- Printing office fee: AED 30

Steps:
1. Choose the preferred application channel
2. Search for the ID card issuance service
3. Fill in the application form
4. Upload required documents
5. Pay the service fees
6. Receive notification about application status

Processing Time: 5 days (24 hours for urgent service)

Available Channels:
- Customer Happiness Centers
- Printing Offices
- Website
- Smart Application


SERVICE 2: RENEWAL OF ID CARD
-------------------------------
Description:
A service whereby an expired ID card is renewed.

Conditions:
- Service is mandatory for UAE Nationals, GCC Nationals, and UAE Residents
- Residents must have a valid residence permit
- Passport must be valid for more than 6 months
- Biometric requirements which refers to fingerprints and signature must be completed if required

Required Documents:
- Personal photo (4.5 x 3.5 cm, white background)
- Passport copy for residents and GCC nationals
- Original agency certificate if someone applies on behalf of the applicant
- Birth certificate for children under 15
- Valid residence permit

Fees:
- UAE Citizens (5 years): AED 100
- UAE Citizens (10 years): AED 200
- GCC Nationals: AED 100
- Residents: AED 100 per year
- E-Services fee: AED 100
- Urgent service fee: AED 150
- Printing office fee: AED 30
- Late renewal penalty: AED 20 per day (up to AED 1000 maximum)

Steps:
1. Choose application channel
2. Search for ID card renewal service
3. Fill application form
4. Upload required documents
5. Pay service fees
6. Receive notifications regarding application status

Processing Time: 5 days (24 hours urgent service)

Available Channels:
- Customer Happiness Centers
- Printing Offices
- Website
- Smart Application


SERVICE 3: REPLACEMENT OF LOST OR DAMAGED ID CARD
---------------------------------------------------
Description:
A service whereby a replacement ID card is issued in case the original card is lost or damaged. The new card will have the same validity as the lost or damaged card.

Conditions:
- Residents must have a valid residence permit
- Passport must be valid for more than 6 months
- Biometric requirements must be completed if required

Required Documents:
- Personal photo (4.5 x 3.5 cm, white background)
- Passport copy
- Original agency certificate if someone applies on behalf of the applicant
- Birth certificate for children under 15
- Valid residence permit

Fees:
- Replacement fee: AED 300
- E-Services fee: AED 100
- Urgent service fee: AED 150
- Printing office fee: AED 30

Steps:
1. Choose application channel
2. Search for replacement ID card service
3. Fill application form
4. Upload required documents
5. Pay service fees
6. Receive notification regarding application progress

Processing Time: 5 days (24 hours urgent service)

Available Channels:
- Customer Happiness Centers
- Printing Offices
- Website
- Smart Application
